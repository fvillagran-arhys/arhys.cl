Privacy Policy for iSISALabs

iSISALabs is a corporate application intended exclusively for authorized users of SISA Limitada. The application provides access to operational, laboratory, inventory, client, work order, invoicing, payment, and digital control information required for business operations.

To provide its services, iSISALabs processes information necessary for authentication, device authorization, operational workflows, and data synchronization. Depending on the functionality used, this information may include login credentials, user identifiers, employee identifiers, device identifiers, client information, work order information, digital control records, receiver names, receiver RUTs, receiver email addresses, inventory photos selected by the user, invoice information, payment records, and other operational data required to provide the service.

iSISALabs does not use collected information for advertising, marketing, profiling, or tracking users across applications, websites, or services. The application does not sell personal information to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens, user session information, and saved credentials may be stored securely on the device using Apple's Keychain services. Operational drafts, pending records, and locally generated information may be stored on the device to support offline operation, interrupted workflows, and synchronization when connectivity becomes available.

Device identifiers may be used to validate authorized devices, enforce security policies, and prevent unauthorized access to corporate resources and business information.

Photos and Media Access

Photos are accessed only when explicitly selected by the user and uploaded as supporting evidence for operational or inventory-related records. The application does not access or scan the user's photo library without user action.

Biometric Authentication

Biometric authentication, when enabled, is processed exclusively by Apple's Local Authentication framework and iOS security services. iSISALabs does not collect, receive, store, or transmit biometric information, fingerprints, facial recognition data, or any other biometric identifiers.

Data Sharing

Information processed by iSISALabs may be shared only with SISA systems, authorized internal users, infrastructure providers required to operate the service, or when disclosure is required by applicable law, regulation, audit, or contractual obligations.

Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to satisfy operational, contractual, legal, security, and audit requirements.

User Rights

Users may request information regarding their data, or request correction or deletion where applicable, by contacting SISA through the information provided below.
